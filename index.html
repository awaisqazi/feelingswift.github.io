Feeling Swift

Feeling Swift is a concept designed by Fez based around the premise that listening to music can be a vital way of expressing emotions. In my life, I am able to remain calm and collected even when dealing with seemingly unbearable circumstances, because the way I manage my emotions is by listening to Taylor Swift songs that make me feel less alone in the way I feel. As a result of this, I have been able to navigate some of the most stressful situations fathomable, all while keeping calm and composed. Not just that, I have also found that those challenging times have been the times where my character has been strengthened the most and I have gleamed so much knowledge just by critically thinking about my challenges and working on resolving them, rather then letting my emotions overwhelm me. I put together the above playlists so that others can also use Taylor Swift's inspiring discorgaphy as a tool to help them get through the challenging moments in life. Just choose what emotion you're feeling and you'll find a playlist that I used to work through that feeling. Hope you enjoy!
